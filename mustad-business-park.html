About this property

More than space — a business ecosystem

Mustad Business Park is not just a place to rent an office. It's a thriving ecosystem of 70 companies across tech, industrial, creative and public sectors — all sharing infrastructure, services and a community that makes business better. Founded in 1832 as the world's largest fish hook factory, the campus has been transformed into a modern business hub while preserving its iconic brick and corten architecture.

Located in Gjøvik's redeveloped industrial zone, the park offers 36,000 m² of mixed-use space — from flexible office suites to warehouses and light industrial units. Whether you're a startup of two or a company of two hundred, the park adapts to your growth without the disruption of relocating.
