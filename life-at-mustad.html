Life at Mustad

More than a place to work — a campus built for community, wellbeing, and collaboration

The Mustad experience

What it feels like to work here

Mustad Business Park isn't just walls and desks. It's a birch-tree terrace where you have lunch in the sun. A cafe that knows your order. A gym you can hit before your morning meeting. Neighbours who become collaborators. Since 1832, this campus has brought people together — today it does the same for 70 businesses and over 800 people.

What's on campus

Facilities & services

Free parking

Generous on-site parking for tenants and visitors, right at your door

Bright Café

On-site canteen and coffee shop with daily lunch menus, fresh pastries, and great coffee

Meeting rooms

Fully equipped AV rooms with interactive screens, bookable by all tenants

Cleaning & maintenance

Professional cleaning, snow removal, and on-call maintenance so you can focus on business

SIT Treningssenter

On-site gym with cardio, weights, and group classes. Available to all tenants at reduced rates

Outdoor & social areas

Birch garden terrace, courtyards, green campus grounds, and event spaces for networking
